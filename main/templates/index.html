Handwriting To Text Converter Using CNN

A handwriting-to-text converter using Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) leverages deep learning to automatically recognize handwritten characters and convert them into machine-readable text. CNNs excel at identifying spatial hierarchies and patterns within images, making them ideal for handwritten text recognition.

By training on large datasets of handwritten samples, the model learns to distinguish various writing styles and nuances, achieving high accuracy in converting diverse handwriting into digital text.

Applications include digitizing historical documents, automating data entry, educational tools, and note-taking apps. CNN-based handwriting recognition is robust, scalable, and continues to improve with more data and advanced training techniques.
